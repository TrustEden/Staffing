# Healthcare Staffing Bridge App – Feature Map

## MVP Scope
- Unified shift board for internal staff and partner agencies
- Facility admins manage shifts, visibility, and approvals
- Agency admins view active facilities, claim and coordinate shifts
- Conflict detection prevents overlapping assignments and warns on tight turnarounds
- In-app notifications for claim lifecycle events
- Excel/CSV import to backfill schedules quickly
- Role-based dashboards (facility, agency, platform admin)

## Phase 2 Ideas
- Invitation-based onboarding for agencies
- SMS/Email notification channels (Twilio / SendGrid)
- Advanced analytics (fill rates, time-to-fill)
- Calendar heat maps and printable summaries
- Automated tiered release scheduler with reminders

## Phase 3 / Scale
- Marketplace discovery for new facility–agency relationships
- Mobile-first experience & offline shift check-in
- Payroll integrations and invoice reconciliation
- Credential tracking and expiration alerts
